2nd Internation Conference On Signal & Data Processing

School Of Electrical And Electronics Enginnering (SEEE)

VIT Bhopal University

June 10-12 2022
Scopus indexed conference in virtual mode

ICSDP 2022 Springer (SCOPUS)

The last decade has witnessed a tremendous growth in various domains of Engineering and Technology. This conference like its previous edition will focus on providing a platform to researchers and scholars of various Engineering discipline to exhibit state of the art research work in thrust areas. The conference will encompass quality research papers in the domain of Artificial intelligence & Machine learning, Internet of things (IoT), Cybernetics, Advanced communication systems, VLSI Embedded Systems, Power electronics and automation, MEMS/ Nanotechnology, Renewable energy, Bioinformatics, Data acquisition and mining, Antenna & RF systems, Power systems, Biomedical engineering, Aerospace & navigation. With this, the innovative and standard results will be disseminated to the researchers in the Engineering and Technology field.

It is planned to publish the peer reviewed and selected papers of conference as proceedings with springer in LNEE series (https://www.springer.com/series/7818)It is planned to publish the peer reviewed and selected papers of conference as proceedings with springer in LNEE series (https://www.springer.com/series/7818).
